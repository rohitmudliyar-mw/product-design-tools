# Be Bodywise — Brand Context for Audit

## Brand Overview
- **Brand**: Be Bodywise (by Mosaic Wellness)
- **Category**: Science-backed skincare, haircare, body care & wellness for women
- **Market**: India
- **Platform**: Mobile-first (iOS + Android) + Web
- **Mission**: Simplify wellness through clear design, honest information, and products that work
- **Products**: Serums, gummies, washes, SPF, supplements, targeted treatments (hairfall, PCOS, underarm care, body skin, sleep)
- **Differentiator**: Science-backed but warm — not cold like Minimalist, not fluffy like Dot & Key. Loyal community over viral moments.
- **Mascot**: "Bella" — used in illustrations and select communications only, never part of the logo lockup

## Voice & Tone

**Primary voice**: Clear, credible, and calm. Scientific clarity with a sense of ease. Informed but never overwhelming. Modern but never trend-chasing.

### Voice Traits
| Tone Trait | Do | Don't |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| Direct | "Apply twice daily." | "Refer to usage instructions." |
| Casual | "Ha baal aajayenge." | "Promotes regrowth naturally." |
| Functional | "Minoxidil. Works only if you use it." | "Powered by clinically proven..." |
| Reassuring | "We got your back." | "Support is always available." |
| Witty | "Not magic. But close." | "Guaranteed miracle!" |

### Voice Keywords
Credible, Supportive, Clear, Clinical, Minimal, Feminine

### Copy Principles
- Science, simplified — distill complex formulations into easy, effective routines
- No overclaims, just outcomes — never chase fads or inflate benefits
- Clarity builds trust — from how we speak to how we show up
- **Never say** "cures", "treats", or "heals" — these are drug claims
- **Avoid** "guaranteed results" or unverified percentage claims

---

## Design System — Iconography

### UI Icons: Lucide Icons
- Stroke weight: **1.5px** — clean, elegant outlines
- **Always outlined, never filled** — keeps visuals light and modern
- Integrates seamlessly with typography and colour palette

### Product/Label Icons
- Monoline with warm neutral tone (cocoa or walnut brown)
- Visual metaphors grounded in real-world objects (serum dropper, razor, cream jar)
- Consistent line weight and spacing

---

## Design System — Photography & Imagery

### Product Photography
- Soft gradients or neutral backgrounds (light gray, off-white, powder blue)
- No props unless they serve a functional purpose
- Skin tones should appear natural and inclusive
- Product label must always be visible and legible
- Premium 3D realistic images using Be Bodywise color palette

### Lifestyle Photography
- Capture people mid-action (grooming, walking, reading)
- Simple, grounded backgrounds
- Real moments, not posed

---

## Design System — Logo

- **Logotype**: Clean, geometric, lowercase. Never recreate using type tools.
- **Mascot**: "Bella" — illustrations and select communications only
- **Safe zone**: Equal to the height of the letter "o" on all sides
- **Minimum size**: Digital: 124px wide
- **Approved backgrounds**: White, black, blue
- **Never**: recolor, add effects, stretch, place over illegible backgrounds

## Target Personas

### Hair Care
**Hairfall Stage 1** (18–25): Mild thinning, wants prevention. Beginner-friendly, "preventive care" messaging.
**Hairfall Stage 2**: Noticeable thinning, needs clinical backing. Guided AM/PM routines, before-after proof.
**Post-Partum Hair Loss**: New mom, extreme hairfall. Safety messaging, baby-safe formulas, real mom stories.

### Body Care
**Dry Cracked Heels**: Fast healing, overnight solutions. Before-after images, "visible results in X days."
**Body Acne/Bumps**: Smoother skin, gentle exfoliation. Ingredient education (AHA/BHA), step-by-step guides.
**Underarm Care**: Odor + pigmentation control. Alcohol-free, climate-specific benefits.

### Sun Protection
**SPF**: No white cast, no oily finish. Lightweight, sweat-resistant, non-comedogenic.

### Wellness
**Nutrition/Supplements**: Active woman, clean-label supplements. Personalised quizzes, ingredient comparison.
**Better Sleep**: Herbal, melatonin-free options. "Better sleep in X days" programs.
**PCOS**: Managing symptoms through lifestyle. Gynaecologist-backed, hormone-balancing nutrition + skincare.

### Customer States (cross-persona)
**Active**: Personalised recs, subscription options, early access.
**Inactive**: "We Miss You" campaigns, win-back incentives.
**Skeptic**: Social proof, money-back guarantees, low-risk entry (samples, first-time discounts).

### Common Traits
- **Age**: 18–35
- **Location**: Urban India (metros + tier-1 cities)
- **Device**: Mobile-first
- **Decision maker**: She decides for herself
- **Influenced by**: Instagram, skincare communities
- **Trust signals**: Ingredient transparency, dermatologist endorsements, real testimonials, clinical backing

## Key Audit Considerations

- Content & Communication weighted highest after core (15%) — complex health topics (PCOS, hairfall stages, hormones) need clear, jargon-free explanation
- Trust is critical (15%) — users are often skeptics who need proof before purchasing
- Clean, minimal design is the brand promise — visual clutter is a brand violation
- **Gambetta (serif) is display/brand font only** — using it in body text or UI is a violation
- Mi Sans is shared across all MW brands — same weights (380/520/630)
- Icons must be **Lucide, outlined, 1.5px stroke** — not filled, not Material Icons
- **Never overclaim** — flag any "cures", "heals", "guaranteed results" copy as P1
- Photography should be inclusive of Indian skin tones — flag Western-default imagery
- Page background is clean white/light — not warm like LJ
- The brand is NOT trend-chasing — flag any "trending", "viral", "everyone's buying" type messaging
- Category accent colors (purple, pink, yellow) are used sparingly — flag overuse
- Empathy is key — flag any condescending, preachy, or lecture-y tone in copy
